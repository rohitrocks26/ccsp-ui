Pagination Component

This component is used to generate numer of pages on the basis of the page's capacity

Feature

  • Data: Number of pages are counted on the basis of number of records in a particaluar page and total number records
  • Event: clicking any page it emits the page count

    • How to Include It

    Selector: paginationComponent
